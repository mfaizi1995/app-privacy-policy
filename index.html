Privacy Policy for Quran App

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Quran App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

  • Reading Progress: We store your last read position locally on your device to help you continue reading where you left off. This data never leaves your device.
  • Audio Cache: Downloaded audio recitations are stored locally on your device for offline listening. This data never leaves your device.
  • App Preferences: Your settings such as font size, theme preference, and selected reciter are stored locally on your device.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not display advertisements

Third-Party Services

Our app connects to the following external services:

Quran.com API

  • Purpose: To fetch audio recitations from various reciters
  • Data Sent: Only the chapter number and reciter selection to retrieve the correct audio file
  • Privacy Policy: https://quran.com/privacy

Data Storage

All user data (reading progress, preferences, cached audio) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers that collect or store your personal information.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device lock features (PIN, fingerprint, face recognition)

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data on external servers, there is no personal data for us to delete, modify, or export. All your app data can be cleared by:

  • Clearing the app's data/cache in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app
